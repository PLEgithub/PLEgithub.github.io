Welcome to Line Gallery!

This site was created as a customized platform where visual content creators can arrange and display their works in an environment curated and stylized to their own liking - even while maintaining their digital assets in other public repositories, like YouTube.

Line Gallery offers a personalized interactive viewing theater experience to present visual works to users, as well as a voting section where users can have their voices heard in regards to which videos they prefer. It also has sections where content creators can house additional information or press materials related to their work (should there be any), or a chance to set up a digital storefront and sell work or merch directly to the users.

Additional “About Me” sections are available to give users more information creators want them to have - on the artist/developer, about the works, tour or geographic information, etc. Currently, these sections provide information on what was used to build the Line Gallery website.

So please click around, relax and enjoy the crappy placeholder videos!
